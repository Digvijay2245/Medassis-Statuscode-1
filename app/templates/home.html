Predictive Models

Understanding Your Health Predictions

Medassis uses advanced machine learning models to provide you with personalized health predictions. Simply input your relevant health data, such as age, lifestyle habits, and medical history as asked into the app. Our models then analyze this information to calculate the probability of conditions like diabetes, kidney disorders, liver cirrhosis, and pneumonia. The result is a clear report that not only indicates whether you’re at risk but also provides a probability score, helping you understand the likelihood of developing these conditions.

FAQs

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our features and services.

How do the predictive models work

Our predictive models analyze your health data, such as age, lifestyle, and medical history, to estimate the risk of conditions like diabetes, kidney disorders, liver cirrhosis, and pneumonia. You receive a report detailing the probability of developing these conditions, helping you take preventive measures.

Is my personal health data safe?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All health information is securely stored in our database, accessible only through your personal login. Your data is protected and visible only to you—no other user can access it.

How can I provide feedback or report an issue?

You can easily submit feedback or report issues through the app. Look for the feedback option in the sidebar, where you can share your thoughts and help us improve Medassis.

What features are included in Medassis?

Medassis offers a range of features including predictive health models, medication reminders, food and medicine analysis, safe eating recommendations, a daily diet chart, a custom AI chatbot access to community support and much more.

How do I get started with Medassis?

Go to Medassis website, create your account, and start by entering your health data. Start exploring the features

Can I use Medassis on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your Medassis account from multiple devices. Your data will sync across all devices where you are logged in, ensuring you have your health information available wherever you go.

Is there a cost to use Medassis?

No, Medassis offers a range of features at no cost
